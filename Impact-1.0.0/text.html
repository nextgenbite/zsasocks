Introduction

ZSA Interweave is a socks knit factory manufacturer and supplier of sports and everyday comfort socks. We manufacture for top brands worldwide, and we offer services that exceed customer satisfaction through continuous improvement. We aim to establish long-term relationships with our clients through ethical, honest, and transparent transactions.

Products and Services

Plain, Terry, Rib, Tennis/Terry, and Jacquard socks for a variety of needs.
Knee High, Half Cushion, Ankle, and 3D Socks tailored to customer preferences.
Custom designs and patterns developed to meet specific requirements.

Our emphasis on quality management and teamwork ensures world-class products at competitive prices, delivering continuous improvement and innovation.

Machineries

We are equipped with 10 state-of-the-art single-cylinder Soosan knitting machines ranging from 72 to 156 needles. Our facilities include:

Automatic linking machine and advanced patterning systems for multi-color options.
Dedicated facilities for quality checks, including iron and finishing units.

Production Capacity

We have a production capacity of 60,000 pairs per month, ensuring reliability and scalability.

Compliance

Our factory is currently in the process of obtaining SADEX certification to meet global standards.
